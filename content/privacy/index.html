Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 15, 2024

I. Information We Collect

i. Information You Provide

We may collect the following information when you interact with our site:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, or other details when subscribing to our mailing list or filling out forms.
  • Messages: Information shared through contact forms or other communication channels.

ii. Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, and time spent on the site.
  • Cookies: Data stored on your device to improve your browsing experience (see Section 5 for details).

II. How We Use Your Information

We may use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our website.
  • To send periodic emails or newsletters (only if you have subscribed).
  • To respond to your inquiries or requests.
  • To improve website functionality and user experience.

III. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties, except:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who help us operate our website or send emails, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law to protect our rights or comply with a legal process.

IV. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or comply with legal obligations. You may request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at support@ari-khan.com.

V. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Understand site usage and improve performance.
  • Remember user preferences (e.g., language settings).

You can control cookie usage through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your experience on our site.

VI. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external websites. Please review their policies before sharing your information.

VII. Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

VIII. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us to have it removed.

IX. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access, correct, or delete your information.
  • Withdraw consent for email communications.
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@ari-khan.com.

X. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.

XI. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
